Malai Kulfi

Description

Kulfi is best described as Indian-style ice cream. However, unlike ice cream, kulfi is not churned. This is a fantastic summer dessert. This is a quick and easy version of kulfi.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup dry milk powder
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste (Optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachio nuts

Steps

  1. Stir the milk, condensed milk, and dry milk powder together in a heavy-bottomed pan and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the sugar, cardamom, and saffron to the boiling milk; reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring frequently, for 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and allow to cool to room temperature; fold the pistachio nuts into the mixture.
  4. Pour the mixture into popsicle molds and freeze until solid, about 1 hour
